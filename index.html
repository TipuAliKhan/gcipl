About GCIPL

GCIPL is civil construction company that has built a strong reputation for delivering a high quality and professional service on time, and at a competitive price.We pride ourselves on being skilled and experienced in all aspects of civil construction and this, combined with our extensive knowledge has established us as major players in this industry.
